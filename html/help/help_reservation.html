How to Make a Hotel Reservation
  • 1. Search for Hotels - Click the "Search Your Destination" button and enter your desired location, check-in/check-out dates, and number of guests.
  • 2. Browse Hotels - View the list of available hotels with their ratings, amenities, and prices. Click on any hotel to see more details.
  • 3. Select a Room - Choose your preferred room type from the available options. Review room details, amenities, and pricing.
  • 4. Review Booking Details - Confirm your booking summary including dates, guest information, and total price.
  • 5. Make Payment - Select your payment method (Touch 'n Go eWallet, Bank Transfer, or Credit Card) and complete the payment.
  • 6. Get Confirmation - You will receive a booking confirmation via SMS and email with your Booking ID.
Cancellation Policy
  • Free Cancellation - Available up to 24 hours before check-in.
  • Late Cancellation - Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a charge equal to one night's stay.
  • How to Cancel - Go to Booking History, select the booking, and click "Cancel Booking".
  • Refund Process - Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Important: Payment must be completed within 24 hours after booking confirmation. Failure to pay will result in automatic cancellation of your reservation. For any issues, contact our customer support.
