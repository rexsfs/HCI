How to Login to LuxeStay
  • 1. Enter Phone Number - On the login page, enter your registered phone number in the provided field.
  • 2. Click Continue - Press the "Continue" button to proceed with the login process.
  • 3. Verify Your Number - You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via SMS. Enter the code to verify your identity.
  • 4. Access Your Account - After successful verification, you will be logged in and redirected to the home page.
New User? Register Here
  • 1. Click Register - On the login page, click the "Register" button to create a new account.
  • 2. Enter Phone Number - Provide your active phone number for registration.
  • 3. Verify OTP - Enter the verification code sent to your phone.
  • 4. Start Booking - Once registered, you can start exploring and booking hotels.

Important: Make sure your phone number is active and accessible to receive OTP verification codes. If you don't receive the code, please check your network connection or contact customer support.
